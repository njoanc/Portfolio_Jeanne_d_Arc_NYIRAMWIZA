Africa Digital Epidemiology Innovation Network

The Africa Digital Epidemiology Innovation Network is a community that provides an opportunity for members to lend their expertise and resources, collaborate, and conduct research for various projects in epidemiology.

Backend Optimization with AdonisJS and MySQL:

As a Backend Engineer, I incorporated AdonisJS and MySQL during the development of the Africa Digital Epidemiology Innovation Network System. This strategic implementation aimed to optimize data accessibility, visualization, and performance, resulting in a remarkable 15% reduction in system response time.

The optimization not only enhanced system efficiency but also contributed to an improved user experience. By leveraging AdonisJS and MySQL, we successfully streamlined data processes, making information more accessible and visually impactful for the community.

This project signifies the commitment to advancing digital epidemiology in Africa, utilizing cutting-edge technologies to empower the community in the fight against diseases and to facilitate meaningful collaboration among experts across the continent.
